Research Focus

Climate Change Impact Assessment

Analyzing precipitation extremes and climate projections across the Mississippi River Basin using NASA's NEX-GDDP-CMIP6 datasets.

Heatwave Analysis

Spatiotemporal characterization of heatwave exposure across historically vulnerable communities using satellite-based estimates.

Coastal Flooding Risk

Evaluating compound effects of precipitation and storm surge on coastal flooding risk, with detailed case studies.

Satellite-Based Precipitation

Comparative analysis of satellite-based precipitation data across CONUS and Hawaii to identify optimal satellite performance.

Machine Learning Applications

Applying LSTM sequence-to-sequence learning models for rainfall-runoff modeling and multi-hazard detection.

Urban Climate Resilience

Assessing urban cooling strategies and evaluating effectiveness of green spaces in mitigating heat exposure.
